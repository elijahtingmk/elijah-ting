The Accountable Dialogue Model™

Our proprietary framework provides a four-part structure for navigating any conversation with precision and purpose.

DATA

Start with verifiable facts. Ground the conversation in reality to remove bias and create a shared understanding.

IMPACT

Articulate the tangible consequences on projects, teams, and timelines. Connect actions to business results.

NEED

Identify the core business requirement that isn't being met. Clarify the "why" behind the feedback.

ACTION

Propose a clear, forward-looking, and measurable action to ensure every conversation ends with progress.
