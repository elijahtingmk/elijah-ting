The DISA™ Framework

A simple, repeatable system for navigating any professional conversation with clarity and purpose. This section introduces the core concepts your team will master.

D

Data

Start with objective, verifiable facts. What a video camera would record, free of judgment or interpretation. This builds trust and a shared understanding of reality.

I

Impact

Connect the data to its tangible consequences. Articulate the effect on the business, team, or project to answer the critical "So what?" question.

S

Strategic Intent

Define your positive, forward-looking purpose for the conversation. This ensures the dialogue is constructive and aimed at a shared successful outcome.

A

Actionable Step

Propose a specific, measurable, and owned commitment. This builds accountability and ensures every important conversation has a clear path forward.
