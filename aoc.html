Course Curriculum

A step-by-step journey to becoming a more effective communicator.

Introduction to Compassionate Communication

Explore foundational concepts and the four key components: observations, feelings, needs, and requests.

Communication That Blocks Compassion

Identify common barriers like moralistic judgments and practice transforming them into compassionate communication.

Observing Without Evaluating

Develop the skill of making objective observations without adding judgments through practical exercises.

Identifying and Expressing Feelings

Build a robust emotional vocabulary and practice articulating feelings accurately and effectively.

Taking Responsibility for Our Feelings

Learn to take ownership of your feelings, understand the needs behind them, and achieve emotional liberation.

Requesting That Which Would Enrich Life

Master making specific, positive action requests and differentiate between requests and demands.

Receiving Empathically

Develop empathic listening skills to understand others and sustain empathy, even in challenging situations.
