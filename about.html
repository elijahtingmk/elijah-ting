The Nonviolent Communication (NVC) Framework

1. Empathic Listening

Focuses on listening with deep presence to understand the feelings and needs behind the words, without judgment.

2. Emotional Awareness

Involves identifying and connecting with one's own feelings, recognizing them as signals of met or unmet needs.

3. Clear Expression

Articulating observations, feelings, needs, and requests clearly and honestly, without blame or criticism.

4. Conflict Resolution

Transforms conflicts into opportunities for connection by focusing on shared human needs rather than competing strategies.
