Safety & Best Practices

Keep Your Data Safe

Never upload confidential company information to free AI tools.

Double-Check Results

AI can make mistakes or "hallucinate" incorrect information. Always verify outputs.

Protect Personal Data

Be careful with employee information and sensitive company details.

Know When to Ask for Help

Recognize when NOT to use AI. Ask your supervisor if you're unsure.
